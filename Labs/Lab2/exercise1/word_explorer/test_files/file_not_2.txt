Circuit Changes and Track Evolution

Several classic venues have undergone modifications to improve overtaking opportunities and enhance race-day excitement. Some layouts have been altered to accommodate evolving regulations, while others have been introduced to bring the sport to new audiences. Street circuits have become more prominent, offering tight corners and dramatic scenery, contrasting with the traditional fast-flowing tracks. Additionally, track surfaces play a crucial role in performance, with resurfacing projects significantly impacting handling and setup. As the calendar expands, new locations bring unique challenges, adding to the unpredictability of modern racing.
